Can we have only the lunch and the coffee roulette?
Of coarse! If you do not have any gaming facilities in, it is possible (and recommended) to just take the lunch and coffee roulette activities!
What do you mean by Fifa?
By Fifa we mean Fifa the video game, most commonly on Playstation of Xbox.
Can we have a custom activity, other than the ones listed?
For now we do not support any additional custom activities, but we are considering this for the future.
Can we have custom branding for our company?
Yes! Custom branding is included in the Pro plan. Send us your company's logo and colour palettes, and we'll apply the look and feel of your company to the Officeroo app.
