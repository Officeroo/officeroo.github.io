What Officeroo does

Officeroo is an app for your office that leverages your office facilities to create fun encounters between employees. It does this using two methods: the Roulette and the League.

1.  The Officeroo Roulette

A roulette is a weekly pairing between two collegues, to meet up for an office activity.

For example, if Alice signs up for Ping Pong, on Mondays she would receive an email like:
"Hi Alice, your ping pong roulette for the week is Bob! Make sure to meet up and challenge him for a game this week :)".

​If ping pong is not Alice's cup of tea, no worries! Officeroo currently supports Pool, Playstation Fifa and Table Football, Coffee and Lunch roulettes. There is something for everyone and many different ways of making new friends!

2.  The Officeroo League

For each gaming activity at your office, the Officeroo League creates a fun ranking system. 

Employees can enter their match scores after every game they play in the app, and see their ranking evolve within their office's League. There is a separate league for Ping Pong, Pool, Table Football and Playstation Fifa (depending on what is available at your office!).

The Officeroo League is a great way to encourage the competitive spirit at your office, whilst creating new encounters!
