What Officeroo does

Officeroo is an app for offices that enables magical connections and fun encounters between employees. It does this using two methods: the Roulette and the League.

1.  The Officeroo Roulette

A roulette is a weekly pairing between two colleagues, to meet up for an office activity.

For example, if Alice signs up for the Coffee Roulette, on Mondays she would receive an email like shown in the example: →

​If Alice is more into office games, no worries! Officeroo currently also supports Ping Pong, Pool, Fifa and Table Football and Lunch roulettes. There is something for everyone and many different ways of making new friends!

2.  The Officeroo League

For each gaming activity at your office, the Officeroo League creates a fun ranking system. 

Employees can enter their match scores after every game they play in the app, and see their ranking evolve within their office's League. There is a separate league for Ping Pong, Pool, Table Football and Fifa (depending on what is available at your office!).

The Officeroo League is a great way to encourage the competitive spirit at your office, whilst creating fun interactions and encounters!
